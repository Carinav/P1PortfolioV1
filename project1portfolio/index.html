about me

Hi, I'm Carina. I'm training to be a full stack developer with iO Academy. Before this I spent 12 years working in the film sector managing cinemas, running industry events and conferences, and working for Gower Street Analytics, a FilmTech company specialising in analysing Box Office data and working with distributors on a global scale to predict box office returns and set film release dates.

I've always loved problem solving and getting to grips with the tech wherever I've worked. Working alongside the great team of devs at Gower Street and seeing their day-to-day work inspired me to train as a dev myself!
